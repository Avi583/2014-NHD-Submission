Primary Sources

 

Books

Amuzegar, Jahangir. Managing the Oil Wealth: OPEC's Windfalls and Pitfalls. 
     London, UK: I.B. Taurius & Co. Ltd., 1999. Print.
     This book shows OPEC's instability (sometimes powerful, sometimes not).

Gardner, Lloyd C. The Road to Tahrir Square. New York, New York: The New Press, 
     2011. 129-130, 135-136, 140. Print.
    The Road to Tahir Square gave me snippets of information on OPEC's influence on the Middle East. *note i did not use the whole book for this project, I only used certain pages.

Pollack, Kenneth M. A Path out of the Desert. USA: Random House, 2008. Print.
     This book talks about how America could depend less on the Middle East, and become more independent.

Skeet, Ian. Opec: Twenty-Five Years of Prices and Politics. New York, New York: 
     Cambridge Energy Studies, 1988. Print.
     This book talks about how OPEC prices are influenced by Middle Eastern politics i.e. higher prices if there is a war in the Middle East.
